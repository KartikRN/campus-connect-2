What is Campus Connect ?

According to the founders Aryan and Kartik , Campus Connect is a centralised platform for all the information regarding a college. Initially built to sell the merchandise for their branch, Campus Connect evolved from just an idea to a full-blown project. During the first year of their college, they were uninformative about the environment of the college, clubs, events and other lots of stuff. So the idea of Campus Connect was generated to let people come at one place and gather all the information the require ,
